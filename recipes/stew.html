Rock's Horneater Stew

Rock's Horneater Stew is a traditional recipe from the Horneater peaks (so called because Horneaters to eat the entirety of chulls, including horns and shells). This recipe is a staple of the Bridge Four crew, prepared by Rock.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Cremlings (shellfish)
  • Soulcast bread
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil the water and crushed tomatoes in a large pot over a campfire.
  2. Add the garlic, spices, sweet potatoes, and cremlings - including the shells, unless you are an airsick lowlander.
  3. Stir until fragrant and cooked through.

When serving to an enemy

In the unlikely scenario where you are serving to a grave enemy, add a large serving of dung to the pot.
