Men's Spicy Curry

This spicy curry is a favorite recipe for Alethi lighteyed men. In Alethkar, men and women eat different cuisines, with men's food often being spicier and women's food often being sweeter.

Ingredients

  • 5 large tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Cumin
  • Sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 pound chull meat
  • 1/4 pound cremlings
  • 2 cups rice

Steps

  1. Saut√© the onion and garlic with cumin and other available spices. Start cooking the rice in the meantime.
  2. Add tomatoes when fragrant.
  3. Add sweet potatoes and cook until they become soft.
  4. Add the chull and cremlings, remove from heat when cooked through.
  5. Divide and serve with rice on a plate.
